ABOUT US

Alterait Builders was incorporated by a few like-minded professionals with a strong desire, vision and experience over 10 years to create an industry-leading organization that would push the boundaries of the local construction scene.
Our main construction activity has traditionally been in housing where we have long and proven track record in executing upgrading as well as new build projects. 
Alterait Builders & Developers (P) Ltd is the trusted builders in Trivandrum. We provide budget villas and residential apartments in and around Thiruvananthapuram. We specializes in interior and exterior designing , Building Supervison and Land Development.

- MISSION -
Our mission is to deliver promises with strong adherence to ethical business practices.Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional service by every member of our team. We provide value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process.

- MOTTO -
Our motto is 'Commitment to deliver the highest quality standards and strive for customer satisfaction.'

- VISION -
We believe in creating projects based on understanding of consumer needs and preferences. Our well thought vision, motivates us to reach up to the customer's expectations and ensure their delight at all times. To be known for our authenticity, hard work and largely excellent quality work. Our Vision "Think big and make dreams a reality ".
